Idk, this is kinda scrapped story

"Onii-chan."

I hear Chiiko's voice calling me. I quickly snap out of my dream of the past.

"What, what?"

Just as we walk into a clearing on the hillside, Chiiko stops and points to the east.

"I think that's what I sensed earlier."

I turn my head to look in the direction. Over the mountainside I see a giant cloud seemingly sucking

all the light around it, producing a dim dark purple glow over the whole east side of the sky. Ever so

slowly approaching us. Inside it thousands of sparks, a beast of electricity throwing lightning in all

directions.

In confusion I ask: "What... is that?"

"The Last General."

Chiiko answers with a terrified look.

"The last what?"

I have no idea what is that supposed to be but when even Chiiko is worried...

"According to the legend..."

Chiiko grabs my hand and starts running. As we pick up the pace she begins explaining.

"... there was a secret weapon being developed during the war. However the world has come to an

end before the scientists had the chance to finish it. It is believed that after seeing that there is no

hope for victory, the last remaining general of the defending forces tried launching the weapon and

got consumed by it in the process."

She slows down into a walk to grab a breath.

"Now he is doomed to forever linger inside it, watching it kill the people he swore to protect."

She breaks into run once again, pulling me with her.

We run up the hill for maybe five minutes before I release Chiiko's hand and stop to yell.

"Wait! Aren't we supposed to be running downhill to avoid it? We're going to be right in the middle

of the storm if we go up!"

"There is no avoiding it, we need to reach the lookout to have a place to hide in."

"But how is that going to be enough to protect us from a storm like this?!"

"This is more than just a storm, it's a weapon. Let's go, onii-chan! Trust me on this one."

I've heard this line few times before and I admit that she ended up being right every single time.

Chiiko indeed knows a lot of things about this world, I wonder how long she's been traveling through

these lands before I met her.

I give in and nod.

And so we continue running up the hill. Chiiko's long pink hair flowing in the air as the wind starts to

pick up. I'm slightly lagging behind, able to observe this beautiful sight. I wish I could forget why we

are running away and just chase this girl for the chance to comb her hair after we exhaust ourselves

and lay in the soft grass. We would just rest there till the evening, watching the sun set into the

distant hills. Just like it always happens in manga. Maybe it would be the day of our first kiss...

"Need a break?"

I snap back into reality as Chiiko interrupts my fantasies. Feeling a bit embarrassed realizing she is

the person I just had these thoughts about, I look to the ground and just murmur "I'm fine" under my

breath as I sprint past her.

"Huh?"

She completely stops in her steps, confused, but a moment later resumes her jog.

After few hours we arrive at our destination. A square two story building made out of concrete that

seems to have survived for centuries. The weather tried weakening it a lot as you can see the

corroded steel reinforcement grid exposed at several parts of the wall, but the whole structure seems

to be holding strong. The entrance is missing a door, just two corroded steel plates lying on the

ground. The windows are also missing any panels, those seems to have been broken ages ago.

As we step inside, the first thing I notice is a set of steep spiral stairs leading to the second floor.

Along the walls there are four sets of long stone tables with benches, attached to the ground. On the

opposite side a small podium just a step above the floor level.

I get a nostalgic feeling, reminiscent of some manga I must have read when I was a kid. But I cannot

recall the name or even what it was about. Just this vivid image of children visiting a lookout with

their parents, a small band playing bizarre instruments to entertain everyone. Maybe some festival

activities of a nearby town. This vision is however quickly disturbed by the upsetting view of the

reality.

Where are young boys these day going to get their first kiss. Lookouts and observatories in manga

were always the place of romance, young couples sneaking up there after the sunset on the last day of

a festival. Stars watching them as they hold hands. Stars that are still there to this day but nobody can

see, locked behind a layer of never ending grey cloud, a ruined atmosphere probably lethal to anyone

getting up there. Wishes of wings to fly into the clouds, ambitions to go above them in hope to one

day reach the stars - now turned into fear and a desire to stay as far as possible from them, to hide

inside steel bunkers deep underground. How is anyone supposed to find romance in such position...

"Let's check the upper floor."

Chiiko says as she approaches the stairs. I drop my bag near the podium and walk towards there too.

"Actually you go first..."

She looks at me while holding her skirt.

"Oh... I wasn't even thinking about that, I swear I wouldn't have looked."

"Alone with a cute girl like me, who knows what devil would possess you if you had the

opportunity~"

She teases me with a mischievous look.

"Cute girl, cute panties... what would I do indeed..."

I murmur quietly, but making sure she can still hear it.

"Just go!"

She says and pushes me towards the stairs with both hands.

"Ok, ok. I'm going."

I quickly walk up to the second floor. The roof there is completely missing, exposing a dark sky

above us. A large veranda with iron railing is overlooking a valley to the west. I approach closer to

look down and through the fog I can see a shape of what seems to be a ruined town.

"We'll look tomorrow, the view should be clear by then."

Chiiko says as she steps besides me, looking into the valley.

"But for now... let's go down. I have to prepare."

I shoot last glance down the hill, almost as a rain check to someone or something. But no sign of

reply comes back.

"Yeah."

I nod in agreement, head towards the stairs and start walking down.

"So how are we going to hide from that thing here?"

I ask and look over my shoulder. But it's already too late when I realize something important...

First I notice Chiiko's smooth legs and for some reason I can't avert my eyes and look even further.

She is indeed wearing pure white panties...

"I will show you w... w... "

I slowly rise my head to meet her gaze and a feeling of guilt fills my mind.

"... where the hell are you looking, onii-chan?!"

She stops and covers them with her skirt. Her face is completely red, I have to say something.

"Eeh... I mean... you know... you said you will show me so I looked!"

Perfect excuse!

"You looked before I even said anything! And I was definitely not talking about this... Why would

you defile me like this?"

Or maybe not...

"What, defile? I mean... I'm sorry... I..."

I'm really not sure if she is being serious or not right now.

"Just move already!"

As she says that I realize that I stopped in the middle of the staircase without even knowing.

Something about that view was so mesmerizing that I lost the sense of reality.

I quickly jump down the remaining stairs and wait for Chiiko to walk down before looking at her

again. From here I watch her unpack some ground sheets from my bag. She takes one of them,

spreads it on the ground and sits down without saying a word.

I need to cover the entrance, the wind is already picking up and we can't stay here with the doors

open like this. Seeing the ground sheets I get an idea, I should try blocking it with a sheet, these are

pretty durable and can be used as a raincoat too. That might be enough. I take one of them and try to

attach it to the frame of the doorway.

"Damn... Just stay there!"

I talk to the sheet as it keeps sliding down when I try to push it between the exposed reinforcements

of the building.

"Can you fetch me some of the tent nails?"

I call to Chiiko who is still sitting in the middle of the room.

"That's not necessary, leave this to me. Come closer to the center"

"But... uuh, ok..."

I try to object at first, but remembering the times she was right and seeing her confident face now is

enough for me to trust her. I follow her instructions and approach the center.

As I come closer, she takes a kneeling posture and begins some kind of ritual. Fingers entwined, she

pusher her hands to her chest and closes her eyes. I have no idea what is happening but all I can do is

keep quiet and watch.

After a short moment, the area around her chest starts to glow with a faint blue light, almost like a

smoke flowing out of her body. It doesn't take long and the whole center of the room is filled with

this aura, slowly but surely expanding.

As the light reaches me and envelops my whole body, I suddenly feel a calm and relaxed atmosphere,

as if all the problems of the world suddenly disappeared.

"SSSSbhooooo!"

Out of nowhere a loud supersonic noise cracks the peaceful moment as a bright blue wave of energy

comes out of Chiiko's hands, pushing her body to the ground as she loses balance.

"Chiiko!"

I scream as I run to help her. The blue aura abruptly returns back to her chest and the room turns dark.

Sliding down near her I try to pick up her body and rest it in my lap.

"Uuuh..."

A weak sound comes from her mouth.

"Are you alright!?"

"I'm... I'm fine onii-chan."

"Are you hurt?"

"No, really, its fine. It doesn't hurt"

She says with a bit more confidence in her voice.

"What was that aura, what happened?"

"I underestimated the power and lost my concentration. I need to do it to protect us, going to try

again now.

"Wait! Can you explain first? What are you doing?"

"I'll tell you after this onii-chan, please trust me for now, we don't have much time"

"Just if you can..."

She looks at me with embarrassed face while getting up from my embrace.

"... if you can onii-chan, please support me and hold my body in place."

She resumes her kneeling posture and rests her back against my chest, relaxing a bit.

I hug her around the waist and felling very nervous I ask:

"Like this?"

She grabs my hands and tightens my grasp even more. My heart is racing, about to jump out of my

chest if it continues like this. She can surely feel it but still remains calm.

"Yeah... don't let me go..."

She says, entwines her fingers and once again pushes against her chest. I try to compose myself a bit

and focus on my task. After few seconds she gets really stiff and I can feel her shaking. I try to

reinforce my hold even more, I must not let her go. A short moment later the familiar blue light starts

flowing out, spreading lightly into the room. She is twitching in my arms but I manage to hold her in

place. I don't remember seeing her move like this when she did it a moment ago. Most likely because

I was dumbfounded from all the stuff happening to even notice.

As the light reaches the walls of the room, a new shade of blue light starts emitting from Chiiko's

chest. This one is a bit darker and seems more compressed. In a thick beam, it flows towards the

windows, the doorway and the staircase, forming a glass-like field and filling the holes leading

outside of this room.

Once all the windows and cracks are shielded with this forcefield, the light blue aura that covers the

whole area moves away from the center and forms a soft layer on the walls and the ceiling of the

room. And with that Chiiko stops trembling and relaxes in my embrace, releasing a big sigh of relief.

"Mmm...hmmm".

She lies down in my lap, breathing shallowly.

"There we go, that should do it."

She whispers quietly.

At that moment I realize that everything went completely silent, any sounds of wind whispering

through the trees or flowing through the cracks in the walls is gone. I try to listen for a while but there

is nothing to be heard. Even the occasional howling of wild animals is no longer there, just the sound

of our breathing and rustling of clothes as we move.

"Was that magic?"

I interrupt the silence.

"Uum... some people call it magic, but the term I use is World Hack."

Chiiko says as she raises from my lap to face me properly.

"World Hack?"

"Yes, it is when I inject excess energy into the world to add a desired effect."

Chiiko starts explaining.

"The world is defined by the energy that operates it, that goes from like atomic level to levels that our

mind is not even able to properly comprehend, energy that is not measurable and similar stuff."

"Me and other 'magicians', as you would call us, don't need to understand that either as we don't

really touch any of that."

"The way World Hack works is not dependent on the energy of the world but on the energy I can

create and put into the world, simulating the energy of the world. That way I am not changing

anything, I am simply adding a layer that I want, the shield in this case. So I can't make stuff

disappear but I can make something appear, for a limited time at least."

"But it is not a simple task and can easily go wrong, as you saw earlier. If I spawn a new source

without moving the other ones first, the world reacts the same way as when two sources of energy

from the world itself collide. I cannot change the physical laws of the world, I have to follow them

just like everyone else."

"Wait, you mentioned other magicians, is there more people who can do this?"

I interject with a question.

"I only know about one other person actively using World Hack, but in theory anyone can use it.

That is..."

"Oh, so I can learn to use this too?"

I interrupt Chiiko mid-sentence.

"... that is everyone except you, onii-chan. You are the only person who can never use this power."

"But it is not the time to explain yet. I am sorry. I promise you will understand eventually, no matter

what happens, you will understand."

"That's fine Chiiko, I would probably cause the next world apocalypse with how I usually mess

things up. It's better I don't know how to use this."

Chiiko's face suddenly changes from a serious and worried expression to a smile.

"Haha, that's quite possible indeed~"

"In the end, that's why you have me onii-chan. I will always be there to protect you."

I can't help but to smile at this point. I am suddenly reminded of the thought I had earlier today when

we were running up the hill, how peaceful that moment was and how much I wanted to just be in that

fantasy world and forget everything else. And now here we are, in probably the most peaceful

moment since I had to leave the bunker. A weaponized storm or whatever it might actually be is

raging outside right now, yet we are safe in this building, protected by Chiiko's magical field.

No sound of the outside can be heard. Just the frequent flashes of light reflecting on the thick blue

barrier are what gives away that there is something actually happening out there.

Yet... we can still be safe in here, I can still enjoy this moment, a cute girl sleeping in my lap. It feels

like I have everything I wanted, at least for today. I have no idea what is going to happen tomorrow, I

don't even know where we are headed in the first place. But all that seems so... irrelevant now.

Chiiko's long hair is spread across the ground sheet, forming a carpet just like fallen cherry blossoms

that I have read so much about but never seen. Perhaps one day we can view such sight, lay down in

there and relax just like now. Chiiko would probably blend with all the pink there, becoming

practically invisible. I remember one last thing from today's fantasy. I burrow my fingers into her hair

and stroke it, gently combing it as the strands of her hair slowly flow through the spaces between my

fingers. It is so soft... how does she even keep it so clean, my hair is always a mess...

awake or not but it doesn't stop me. I keep combing it for a short while, gradually slowing down after

each stroke. And with a pleasant feeling in my heart I eventually lose consciousness...
